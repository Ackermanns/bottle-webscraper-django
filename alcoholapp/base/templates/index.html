About me

This entire webproject is a assignment for BCDE214 at ARA institute of Canterbury (Polytech). This is for my studies in software engineering bachelors where I pretend to be employed by a client that asked me to make this using a design thinking development process. This project tests my full-stack development where I experiment with different technologies, ideas and media.
Please Note:
The goal of this project is to extract alcohol product data published from owner shops, store and sort them by cheapist price and present them to the user as information that they can do what they want with.
I am not in any way associated with the alcohol shops businesses or am sponsored. Web scraping technology is not illegal and all data that is collected and published here is information published from the owners stores.
